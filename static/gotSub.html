In order to start listening for video content, please setup your VLC player by following the steps shown below

  1. Choose Media/Open Network Stream
  2. In address, enter udp://@224.50.50.50:4444. Don’t forget to enter the “@” symbol after “udp://” and before the multicast ip address!
  3. Click "Play"
